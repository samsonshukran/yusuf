الرحمن
Ar-Raḥmān
Meaning
The Most Gracious
Evidence
Qur'an 1:3, 55:1
Reflection
Allah's mercy encompasses all of creation, believers and non-believers alike.
When to Use
When seeking mercy, when feeling hopeless, when asking for forgiveness
Du'a Example
Yā Raḥmān irḥamnī
Tajwīd Note
The letter ḥ (ح) is a deep throat sound, not like English 'h'
Related Names
Ar-Raḥīm, Al-Ghafūr, Al-Wadūd

walillāh al-asmāʼ al-ḥusnā fadʻūhu bihā
